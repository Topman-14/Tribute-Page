Fela Anikulapo Kuti

So, Who is Fela Kuti?

paraBorn Olufela Olusegun Oludotun Ransome Kuti on the 15th of October 1938 in Ogun State, Nigeria, the Nigerian multi-instrumentalist, bandleader, composer, political activist, and Pan-Africanist is regarded as the pioneer of Afrobeat - an African music genre that combines traditional Yoruba percussion and vocal styles with American funk and jazz. At the height of his popularity, he was referred to as one of Africa's most "challenging and charismatic music performers".
